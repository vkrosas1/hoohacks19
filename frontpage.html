The Hope JAR

The Hope J(ustice)A(nd)R(eform) is a project that seeks
to provide ex convicts a means of restarting their life
after finishing their prison sentence. It allows them to
finish a certification/module that shows that they have
reformed and have the qualifications to work for employers.
The website gives employers a place to post jobs and ex
convicts a place to post their job profiles. The website
then matches the employer/employee based on the criteria.
The project is also a means of educating the public on
prison reform movements so that other people can make a
difference as well. Our mission is to make a positive
impact on the ex-convicts life by giving them a second
chance while educating people on the injustices of the
prison system.
