The Hope JAR

The Hope JAR's(Justice and Reform) mission is to provide an easy way for ex-convicts to
find jobs post serving their sentence. We hope to educate
people on prison reform and provide employers to find
good fits for their jobs. After the convict is done serving his/her
sentence we have them complete a module/certification
that asserts that they have finished their sentence and shown
good behavior and are ready to start their new life. We want our
system to give hope to people and provide them with a means for
restarting their life in a positive manner.
